What does it mean to "Ditto LeadTrader?"

Tags: Follow LeadTrader

First and foremost, you gain the ability to take part in our actual trades. Instead of simply mirroring our trades, Ditto gives you complete control over how you do this.

Here are some of the ways you can modify your settings

  1. You can switch to Ditto Off. It is completely your decision to Ditto our trade or not.
  2. Detach Positions you Already Own
  3. Modify your settings for position sizing
  4. Complete Transparency. You can view position-status 24/7

Getting Started

Tags: Setup

Subscribing to LeadTrader Service

  1. Click "Sign-Up"
  2. Complete Payment Information
  3. Wait for Subscription Confirmation
  4. Connect with LeadTrader on Ditto Trade

During the initial setup of your Ditto Trade Accoount:

  1. Set Investment Objective to Aggressive Growth
  2. Have margin approval
  3. Be approved to trade options
  4. When funding your Dittto Trade account please remember to set it to a minimum of $25,000

Once your connection has been confirmed, you will receive further instructions regarding how to setup your position sizing

  • Once you complete adjusting your position sizing settings, you have everything you need to Ditto - away!
  • Download the Ditto Trade Mobile App to be even more connected to LeadTrader!
